AFC Ajax

Founded in 1900, based in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

Home stadium: Johan Cruyff Arena.

Nickname: de Godenzonen (Sons of the Gods).

Won 4 Champions League titles.

Birthplace of Total Football philosophy.

Youth academy is one of the best in the world.

Rivalry: De Klassieker vs Feyenoord.

Produced legends like Cruyff, Bergkamp, Van der Sar.

Known for discovering and selling young talent.

Dominates the Dutch Eredivisie.

Top 3 players: Johan Cruyff, Marco van Basten, Dennis Bergkamp.
