FC Barcelona

Founded in 1899, based in Barcelona, Spain.

Home stadium: Camp Nou

Nickname: Blaugrana.

Won 5 Champions League titles.

Club motto: Més que un club translates to (More than a club).

Famous for tiki-taka style, especially under Guardiola.

Strong identity with Catalan culture and politics.

Fierce rivalry: El Clásico vs Real Madrid.

famous for the La Masia academy produced Messi, Xavi, Iniesta

One of the world’s most popular clubs.

Top 3 Legends: Lionel Messi, Ronaldinho, Xavi.
