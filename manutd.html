Manchester united FC

Founded in 1878 (as Newton Heath, renamed Manchester United in 1902), based in Manchester, England.

Home stadium: Old Trafford ("The Theatre of Dreams").

Nickname: The Red Devils.

Won 3 Champions League titles.

Most successful English club domestically with 20 league titles.

Historic rivalry with Liverpool and Manchester City.

Known for Sir Alex Ferguson’s era of dominance (1986–2013).

World-famous youth academy (Class of ’92: Giggs, Scholes, Beckham, Neville brothers).

Massive global fanbase, one of the biggest clubs in the world.

Top 3 legends are ronaldo, wayne roney, and Ryan Giggs
