FC Porto

Founded in 1893, based in Porto, Portugal.

Home stadium: Estádio do Dragão.

Nickname: Dragões (Dragons).

Won 2 Champions League titles.

Historic rivalry with Benfica and Sporting (O Clássico & Derby).

Produced and developed top talent, often sold to Europe’s biggest clubs.

Famous Champions League win in 2004 under José Mourinho.

Top 3 players are Deco, Ricardo Carvalho,Radamel Falcao
